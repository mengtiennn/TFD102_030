Josh Divine
ABOUT
Josh Divine is a toy designer and artist from Longmont, CO.  His work has appeared in numerous publications and physical forms.  Most notably as a cartoonist for Playboy and as Toy Designer and Art Director for Kidrobot.Josh has taught classes on character design and construction, animation and entrepreneurship, and volunteers at elementary schools teaching kids about art.  He is currently working on a large series of paintings and is continuing his illustration and design work.
