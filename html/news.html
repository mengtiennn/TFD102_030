Sticky Monster LAB

Sticky Monster Lab, a multidisciplinary creative studio founded in 2007, consists of members with various creative backgrounds. They have produced and released short animations that contain everyday stories in the monster world, which arouses sympathy by reflecting our reality.

KAWS Expanded Holiday

In collaboration with Acute Art, KAWS produced EXPANDED HOLIDAY, which demonstrates the enormous potential of augmented reality AR and conveys a sense of mischievous humor through the juxtaposition of physical and virtual worlds. The app features a small version of COMPANION EXPANDED and AT THIS TIME EXPANDED from his project EXPANDED HOLIDAY, and a series of AR drawings.
